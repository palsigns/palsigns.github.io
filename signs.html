Illuminated signage

Built to glow

A sign is engineering and emotion at once. We draw the letterforms, choose the lighting, build the cabinet, and tune the whole thing until your name reads from the far end of the street.

The difference

Light is the material.

Anyone can cut a letter. The craft is in the glow: the depth of the return, the diffusion of the face, the exact warmth of the LED behind it. We obsess over the parts nobody names but everybody feels when a sign simply looks right after dark.

How it happens

From sketch to switch on.

STEP 01

Survey

We visit your space, measure the elevation, check sightlines, and handle permits and landlord approvals.

STEP 02

Design

We draw your sign to scale, refine the letterforms, and show you realistic day and night mockups.

STEP 03

Fabricate

We build it in our own shop: cutting, forming, wiring, and testing the illumination before it ships.

STEP 04

Install

Our crew mounts, powers, and aligns the sign, then we switch it on and make sure it is perfect.

Materials & lighting

Aluminum returns, cast and routed acrylic faces, marine grade wiring, and energy efficient LED modules rated for the long haul. We build for weather, for code, and for the day a decade from now when your sign still looks like opening night.
